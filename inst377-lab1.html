This is Oshawott, a sea otter Pokémon. 🦦

Oshawott is a water type Pokémon. He is good against fire type Pokémon and weak against grass type Pokémon.

When Oshawott evolves into Dewott, he grows two scalchops on his arms which he uses as swords.
